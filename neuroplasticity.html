Research Growth Mindset

Discuss what it is and why it is relevant

Growth mindset is essentially the philosophy of how to best learn and conceptualise skills, and how to grow emotionally and critically as a person. It's based on our new knowledge of neuroplasticity. Basically, the idea is that our brains can get better at pretty much any skill, as opposed to the idea that everyone has a natural amount of talent for each thing in life, and that talent is fixed, and no amount of effort on our part can change this. It's relevant because what we're learning in Dev Academy is difficult, complex, multi-faceted and conceptual; i.e. it requires a different kind of thinking than we usually use in our lives, and the kind of skills that you hear people say 'I'm not a natural at this' about.
